Beyond BIO

Beyond BIO

Started young

I got my first taste of sales at the age of 15, selling everything from candy to non-alcoholic beer—and consistently overachieving my targets. From day one, I was wired to chase goals and love the hustle.

Became a Managing Director at 30

At the age of 30, I stepped in as Managing Director of a high-performing organisation. Let’s just say—there is a story behind that leap, and it shaped the way I lead to this day.

Global by nature

I have lived and worked across four continents, and every place has added a layer to who I am. Different teams, time zones, and cultures have taught me how to connect, adapt, and lead with empathy.

Chief Party Officer (unofficial title)

I throw legendary parties. From themed nights to impromptu gatherings, I love curating experiences that bring joy, laughter, and connection.

Foodie & kitchen boss

I am an outstanding cook and passionate foodie. I love exploring new cuisines and recreating them in my kitchen with a bit of flair (and sometimes, spice!).

Famous in the toddler/kiddie scene

I sing a lot of kids’ songs and have a fanbase of little humans who think I am one of them—which, honestly, is one of the greatest compliments.

Perpetual learner

I love being in the classroom. From strategy certifications to just learning for the joy of it, I find inspiration in education and believe curiosity keeps you sharp.

Nature over notifications

When I truly want to unwind, I go completely off-grid. No screens, no buzz—just quiet, nature, and long walks in beautiful places. Travel is my reset button.

My favourite team

When I am not working, cooking, or running campaigns, I am hanging out with my favourite people—my husband and our son. They are my biggest cheerleaders, best sounding boards, and constant source of laughter (and reality checks). We often turn our living room into a dance floor—or a racetrack—depending on the mood. There is usually music, chasing, and a lot of giggling involved.
